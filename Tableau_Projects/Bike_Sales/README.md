-Bike Sales Dashboard-

https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/farrel.rafi.aryatama/viz/BikeSales_17657112155790/SalesDashboard

Tools: Tableau

Dataset Source: Public Dataset

Objective:

  Create a dashboard that shows profit, YoY% change, revenue and it's trend line, product COGS, top 10 products sold, product category sales by gender, ratio of total sales by age group and product subcategory, and ratio of total sales by country, all can be filtered by year and countries.

Key Insights:

  • Identified Water Botttle as the primary profit driver

  • Discovered profit concentration in USA and Australia

  • Analyzed Year on Year percentage change
  
Skills Demonstrated: Dashboard design, KPI analysis

In this project, I've built a bike sales dashboard in Tableau. Some visualizations that I've displayed are profit, YoY% change, revenue, and product cost cards, bar charts, stacked column chart, bubble chart, and map. I also added a year parameter as a single-list filter and countries filter.
