-Electric Vehicle Sales Dashboard-

https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/farrel.rafi.aryatama/viz/ElectricVehicleDashboard_17648497328430/Dashboard1?publish=yes

Tools: Tableau

Dataset Source: Public dataset

Objective:

  Create a dashboard that shows ratio of battery electric vehicle vs plug-in hybrid electric vehicle sales, car make sales, top 10 counties with highest sales, sales ratio across the state, and sales across year, all categorized by car make.

Key Insights:

  • Identified BEV dominance over plug-in hybrids

  • Found Tesla’s overwhelming market share

  • Analyzed rapid post-2021 growth in EV registrations
  
Skills Demonstrated: Dashboard design, KPI analysis

In this project, I've created an electric vehicle sales dashboard in Tableau, specifically for EV sales in Washington, USA. I added some column charts, donut chart, bar chart, map, as well as parameter as filters.
